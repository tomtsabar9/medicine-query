Hey There!

This search query is based on the Israeli consumer medicine information (CMI) leaflet, also known as package ‎leaflet.‎ This non-profit website allows a free quick search and download of leaflets by entering English or ‎Hebrew drug name.

How it works?

The site works using the best effort method: First it will look for a consumer ‎leaflet in English, then in Hebrew and if it still does not find any, it will look for a physician leaflet. The ‎leaflets are downloaded from an online drug database of Israel Ministry of Health.
